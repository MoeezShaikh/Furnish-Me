Architecture

01

Architecture is the art and science of designing and constructing buildings and other physical structures. It encompasses the planning, designing, and construction of spaces that serve various purposes, ranging from residential dwellings to commercial buildings, public spaces, and monumental structures.

Interior

02

Interior design is the art and science of enhancing the interior spaces of a building to create a functional, aesthetically pleasing, and harmonious environment that meets the needs and desires of the occupants
